Welcome to The Webpage Maze!

Take a path to try and reach the end of this awful, amazing, creative, boring, colourful, abysmal, repetitive, straight-forward, twisty-turny maze.

Paths are how you traverse this maze. These paths can be found in all shapes and sizes. Almost anything and everything should be suspected of being a path!

In this maze, you will go through several different pages to try and find an end of the maze. The pages you pass by could be any type of mock-up. Fake stores, random websites, etc. Basically, click things to find an end, then do it again until you've collected as many endings as you can find!
